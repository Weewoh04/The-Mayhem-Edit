# Content Guide

## Collection Title Formulas

- `Gifts for [Person] Under [Budget]`
- `[Occasion] Gifts That Are Actually Useful`
- `The [Lifestyle] Kit`
- `Easy [Event] Outfit Formula`
- `[Room or Routine] Must-Haves`

## Pinterest Image Sizes

Use `1000 x 1500` pixels for pins. Keep text large, leave breathing room, and show the collection theme clearly.

## Product Count

Aim for 4 to 8 products per guide. That is enough to be useful without turning the page into a cluttered storefront.

## Product Descriptions

Write one sentence explaining why the product belongs in the edit:

```text
A hands-free bag that keeps errands, school pickup, and quick trips easier.
```

Avoid copying Amazon descriptions or reviews.

## Avoid Thin Affiliate Content

Each collection should include a real point of view:

- Who the guide is for
- Why the products work together
- When each item is most useful
- Practical notes about sizing, storage, routines, or gifting
- Related collections for the next shopping step

## URL Slug Format

Use lowercase words separated by hyphens:

```text
teacher-gifts-that-are-actually-useful
```

Do not use dates, punctuation, or vague slugs like `post-1`.

## Image File Naming

Use the same slug style:

```text
teacher-gifts-that-are-actually-useful.jpg
teacher-gifts-that-are-actually-useful-pin.jpg
insulated-tumbler.jpg
```

Keep files organized in:

- `public/images/collections/`
- `public/images/products/`
- `public/images/pinterest/`
